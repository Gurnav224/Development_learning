Transportations In Mumbai

Local Trains

Mumbai's lifeline, the local train network, is one of the busiest in the world, connecting the city's suburbs.

Best Buses

The Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport (BEST) buses provide an extensive transportation network within the city.

Metro

Mumbai Metro is a rapid transit system that continues to expand, easing transportation within the city.

Taxis

Mumbai Taxis are a great option for transportation, easing travel within the city.
