Notable Achievements

  • Most Runs in International Cricket: Sachin Tendulkar holds the record for the most runs in both Test and ODI cricket.
  • 100 International Centuries: He is the only cricketer to have scored 100 international centuries (51 in Tests and 49 in ODIs).
  • First Double Century in ODIs: Tendulkar was the first player to score a double century in One Day Internationals.
