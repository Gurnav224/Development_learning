Biograpy

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is an Indian former international cricketer who captained the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket.

Achievements

Cricket Records

Sachin Tendulkar is the highest run scorer in both formats, having amassed an incredible total of 15,921 runs in Test matches and 18,426 in One-Day Internationals.

International Honours

Sachin Tendulkar's impact on cricket and his ability to capture the hearts of cricket fans from various backgrounds earned him the nickname "God of Cricket."
