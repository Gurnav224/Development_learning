Notable Achievements

  • Led the Indian Independence Movement: Mahatma Gandhi was the foremost leader in the struggle against British colonial rule, leading India to independence in 1947.
  • Promoted Non-ViolencePioneered the philosophy and practice of non-violent resistance (Satyagraha), inspiring civil rights movements worldwide.
  • Championed Social Reforms: Worked tirelessly for the abolition of the caste system, upliftment of the untouchables, and promotion of rural self-reliance
