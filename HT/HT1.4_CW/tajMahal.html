About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh

It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaj Mahal and is renowned for its stunning white marbal architecture
